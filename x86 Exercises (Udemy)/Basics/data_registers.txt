Data Registers = ax, bx, cx, and dx
bl is the lower side of the bx register
Only ax, bx, cx, and dx registers can be divided into higher/lower segments. Separated into two registers. Each register would have 8 bits. 

There are 14 registers, and each register has 16 bits. 
ax can be divided into ah and al
bx can be divided into bh and bl
cx can be divided into ch and cl
dx can be divided into dh and dl
